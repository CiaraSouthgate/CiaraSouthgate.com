September 2018 CST Co-op Grades

I put out a survey asking students who got into co-op this term to submit their grades so future students have an idea what to aim for to get in, as this intake was the first with students at two campuses and with two new courses (COMP 1712 and COMP 1930).

The survey had 41 responses (out of 50 total co-op students). 63% were students from the Burnaby campus and 37% were from downtown. Both campuses had the same overall average (~90.5%) for COMP 1510, despite downtown learning in Python and Burnaby learning in Java.

Sorry if the charts are weird. I'm a programmer, not a statistician and I wanted them to have the same scale. And of course, take this with a grain of salt because I have no way of knowing if all the grades are genuine!
